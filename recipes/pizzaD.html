Pizza Dough Recipe

Home

Description

The following recipe is for making pizza dough and for you to add any type of sauce on top of it, it is encouraged to use mozzarella cheese in addition of pepperoni as toppings, but everything goes!

Ingredients for dough

  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven)
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Proof the Yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Prepare the pizzas
